The first planet he visited had a king who liked to give orders.
And according to him, he could order anything to happen. He
could even decide when it was dawn and when it was dusk, just
by asking. The little prince asked him to see a sunrise, the king
told him to wait 10 minutes, what was which was the time that lasted
each night and each day on that planet leaving the little
prince puzzled.
